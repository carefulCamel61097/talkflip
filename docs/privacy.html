Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

ConvoGo ("the app", "we") is a face-to-face translation app for two people sharing one phone. We built it to be private by default. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information.

The short version

  • No account, no sign-up, no advertising, no tracking.
  • We don't sell your data, and we don't build a profile of you.
  • Your conversations are not stored on our servers and are cleared from the app when you close it.

What the app uses, and why

Microphone & speech

When you tap a language to speak, the app uses your device's microphone and its built-in speech recognition to turn your speech into text. This transcription is handled by your device's operating system (Apple). Depending on your device and the language, some audio may be processed by Apple to provide recognition, in accordance with Apple's privacy policy. ConvoGo does not record, save, or upload audio.

Translation

To translate, the app sends the transcribed text (and the two language codes, e.g. "en" and "es") over an encrypted connection to our translation service, which forwards it to the Google Cloud Translation API and returns the translation. These requests are not tied to your name, account, or a device identifier. We do not store the text you translate. Google processes the text to perform the translation under its own terms; see Google's privacy policy.

Usage limits

To keep the service within its free tier, our server keeps a single running count of the total number of characters translated each month. This is an anonymous aggregate number only — it contains no text, no personal data, and nothing that identifies you or your device.

Your settings

The two languages you choose are saved locally on your device so the app remembers them next time. They are not sent to us.

What we don't collect

  • No names, emails, or accounts.
  • No location data.
  • No contacts, photos, or files.
  • No advertising identifiers or analytics SDKs.
  • No conversation history — nothing is stored or synced.

Service providers

Delivering translation relies on a few infrastructure providers who may process technical data (such as your IP address) only as needed to route and serve requests:

  • Apple — operating-system speech recognition.
  • Google Cloud Translation — translates the submitted text.
  • Cloudflare — hosts the lightweight translation proxy.

Each handles data under its own privacy policy.

Children

ConvoGo is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update this page and the "last updated" date above.
