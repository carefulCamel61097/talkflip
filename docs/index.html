ConvoGo

Two people, one phone, one moment. Speak naturally and the person across from you reads your words in their language.

One phone. Two languages. Zero friction.

  • Tap your language and start talking — your words appear as you speak.
  • Tap or swipe to hand the conversation over; ConvoGo translates instantly.
  • Both sides stay on screen like a chat, so you can always glance back.
  • No record or stop buttons. Switching speakers is all it takes.
  • Read, don't listen — fast and clear, even in noisy cafés and stations.
  • 30+ languages, searchable in a tap.

Private by design

  • No account, no sign-up.
  • Conversations live only on your screen and clear when you close the app.
  • ConvoGo asks only for microphone access, to transcribe what you say.
